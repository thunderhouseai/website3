# Art direction

Reference aesthetic: loudsrl.com. Massive display type, vast intentional
negative space, one accent element per view, content anchored off-center
(lower-left third default). Motion is slow and scarce.

Tiebreaker rule: when a visual decision is unspecced, choose bigger type,
fewer elements, more space. Never fill space — use it.

Gradient rule: never blend amber into navy across a full field (produces
olive). Accent color appears as contained glows or on discrete elements only.
