# Art direction
<!-- v2, rewritten at redesign kickoff. Previous LOUD-era version preserved
     in git tag v1-loud-attempt. -->

Reference: thepatchsystem.com/ai — full experience: scroll-driven
narrative, punchy transition beats ("there's a better way" moment),
numbered module walkthrough with visuals, strong rhythm section to
section.

ThunderHouse skin: navy base, bolt amber/orange accents, grays/silvers ok.
Modern with bang — bold type, real energy, scroll-triggered reveals — but
premium and trustworthy, never gimmicky or template-like.

Hero includes a video slot: spec it with a designed placeholder (poster
frame from brand palette) so real video drops in later without a rebuild.

No marquee star-strips. No pricing tables.

Out of scope (recorded here; carry into every stage's out-of-scope notes):
chatbot and FAQ explicitly deferred to a future run.
