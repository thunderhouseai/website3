## Positioning

ThunderHouse AI is a bilingual (EN/ES) AI Digital Strategy company for small
and medium businesses. We build websites, chatbots, automations, apps, and
marketing systems — and we guide businesses on where AI actually creates
value.

Philosophy (verbatim, appears once, prominently, never paraphrased):
"AI is not the strategy — solving business problems is. Use AI where it
makes sense, people where they matter most."

## Audience

SMB owners. Orlando/Central Florida first, then US + Puerto Rico.
Hispanic-market trust matters: dependable, experienced, human. Spanish is a
first-class language, not a translation afterthought.

## Traffic reality

Cold — agentic outreach (spec-built websites sent to businesses without
one) + future Meta/Google ads. The site must validate the outreach:
visitors arrive skeptical, checking if we're legit.

## One action

Book a discovery call (Calendly). Every section funnels here.

## Engagement model (site narrative mirrors this)

Discovery call → understand the business and goals → we build a concrete
plan with a price → you buy it or you don't → we build → small monthly
retainer keeps it running. No packages, no pressure.

## Voice

Source examples (verbatim from founder — extract cadence, vocabulary, sentence length):

Most businesses know they should be using AI, but they do not know where to
start—or how to turn experimentation into something their teams can actually
use. ThunderHouse begins by understanding the business: its goals, pain points,
workflows, people, and systems. We identify where AI can create measurable
value, then design and implement practical solutions around the way the
organization really operates.

ThunderHouse is not here to add another tool to your technology stack. We build
and manage governed, AI-assisted operating systems that turn scattered
knowledge, manual processes, and business conversations into coordinated
action. AI works where it creates leverage, people remain in control where
judgment matters, and every solution is tied to a real business outcome.

Never sounds like: "AI hype / revolutionize everything", "another tool for your
stack / SaaS feature-speak", "corporate consulting filler".
