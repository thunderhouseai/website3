# thepatchsystem.com/ai — observed mechanics (reference material)

Captured Jul 2026 (v2 redesign reference; replaces loudsrl-analysis.md,
which is retained for history only). Patch sells an AI patient-acquisition
system to clinics; the /ai page is a single scroll-driven narrative. This
file records the observed structure. It is reference material, not a spec —
stage 01 turns the in-scope subset into the decomposition.

## 1. Scroll narrative arc

One page, one story, in order: problem recognition → better-solution pivot
→ numbered feature walkthrough → social proof → pricing → FAQ → final CTA.
Each section is a distinct "act" with its own rhythm; generous vertical
padding between acts; scroll-triggered reveals.

## 2. Hero

Bold claim headline ("The most intelligent way to get more patients") +
one-line amplifier ("The proven Patch System, amplified with AI"). Nav
above with section links (What we do, Process, Case Studies, Services) and
a Book a Call / Book a Demo CTA. No language switcher exists on Patch.

## 3. Pain-mirror section

Names the visitor's problems in their own words, one beat per line, each
with an icon: "Leads sit untouched in your inbox" / "Follow-ups slip
through the cracks" / "Revenue fluctuates month to month" / "You've
considered hiring an admin... but that's expensive and messy."

## 4. Pivot beat

A hard turn: "There's a Better way" — immediately followed by a compressed
three-part promise ("Admin Eliminated / Revenue Elevated / Completely
Automated"). This is the page's emotional hinge between pain and solution.

## 5. Numbered module walkthrough (01–05)

Five feature modules as cards, each with number, title, descriptive copy,
benefit statements, and imagery (mobile-optimized). The numbering gives the
walkthrough a sense of a complete, finite system rather than an open-ended
service list.

## 6. Social proof pattern

Repeating banner strips with star icons between sections ("AI admin
responds in minutes", "you always know exactly who's being followed up").
NOTE: ThunderHouse art-direction.md explicitly bans marquee star-strips —
observed, not adopted.

## 7. Pricing + FAQ

Three-tier pricing table and an FAQ accordion near the end. NOTE: both
excluded for ThunderHouse (art-direction.md: no pricing tables; FAQ +
chatbot deferred to a future run) — observed, not adopted.

## 8. Repeated CTA pattern

The same action re-asked at natural decision points: nav (Book a
Call/Demo), "Inquire on Pricing" 3+ times mid-page, "Apply for patchAI"
mid-page, "Book a call" in the footer. One action, many doors.

## 9. Visual style notes

White background with gradient accents (Patch's palette — ThunderHouse
re-skins to navy base + bolt amber/orange, grays/silvers ok). Bold display
headlines over scannable body copy; card-based modules; diagonal-arrow
button icons; strong section-to-section rhythm; motion is scroll-triggered
reveals, not ambient animation.

## 10. Deltas for ThunderHouse (from art-direction.md + site-job.md)

- Adopt: scroll narrative arc, pain mirror, pivot beat, numbered modules
  (our five service modules), repeated single-action CTA (book a discovery
  call), strong rhythm, scroll-triggered reveals.
- Add (Patch doesn't have): hero video slot with designed poster
  placeholder; founder-proof section (Patch leans on testimonials; we lean
  on the founder's 20+ years + named client work); how-it-works section
  narrating the engagement model; EN/ES language switcher.
- Exclude: marquee star-strips, pricing tables, FAQ, chatbot (deferred).
